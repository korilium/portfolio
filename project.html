Peccon Project

A platform for the exchange of knowledge, experience, tools and products in financial planning. It is to provide a space where people can share their insights, ask questions, and learn from each other. The project stands on four main pillars.

Literacy: Providing resources that can help individuals make informed decisions about their finances.

Planning tools: Offering tools that can help individuals create and manage their financial plans.

The fund: Gain access to the financial products and services by becoming owner of the platform.

Products: Developing financial products with community driven features.
