Peccon is a project that aims to create a platform for the exchange of knowledge, experience, tools and products in the field of pensions. The goal is to provide a space where professionals can share their insights, ask questions, and learn from each other. The platform will include features such as forums, articles, and webinars, all focused on the topic of pensions.

The logo of Peccon is a stylized representation of the Greek goddess Athena, who is often associated with wisdom and knowledge. The logo features a minimalist design, with clean lines and a modern aesthetic. The use of blue and white colors reflects the themes of trust and professionalism, while the overall design conveys a sense of innovation and forward-thinking.

The bull is a symbol of strength and determination, representing the resilience and tenacity of the Peccon community. The bull is often associated with financial markets, where it symbolizes a bullish trend or optimism. In the context of Peccon, the bull represents the collective strength of professionals working together to improve the pension landscape.

The stars in the Peccon logo represent the guiding light of knowledge and experience. They symbolize the aspiration to reach new heights in the field of pensions, as well as the idea of collaboration and shared learning. The stars also evoke a sense of community, as they remind us that we are all part of a larger constellation of professionals working towards a common goal.

The background of the Peccon logo is a representation of the digital landscape in which the platform operates. It features a network of interconnected lines and nodes, symbolizing the flow of information and ideas within the community. The background design reflects the modern, tech-savvy nature of Peccon, as well as its commitment to leveraging technology to enhance the pension experience.
